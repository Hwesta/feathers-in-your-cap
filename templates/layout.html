Feathers in your Cap is a birdwatching achievements site powered by eBird.

Birding already has one major achievement: the lifelist! We're just adding a few more. Have you seen all the common birds in your area? How about a bird from every taxonomic order? It's harder than you might think! How many national birds have you seen in that country?

Want to know what achievements you've earned? Want to know how you compare to your birding friends? Sign up and find out!

Why?

Feathers in your Cap was created by Dale Floer and Holly Becker. We're both video gamers, and soon after we got into birding we knew it was missing something: achievements! Since achievements other than lifelist tracking didn't exist, we decide to create it.
